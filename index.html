Age of 6ixmar

Age of 6ixmar is a Toronto based gaming group dedicated to the tabletop miniatures game "Age of Sigmar" from Games Workshop.
We run tournaments throughout the GTA and Durham. To find and event near you please check our events page!
While we're focused primarily on the AoS competitive scene we still like to reach out an invite new players in join in the action. Whether it's painting, the hobby, or Matched Play, Age of 6ixmar has something for everyone
